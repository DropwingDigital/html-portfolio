The Best Movies According to Tanika

My top 3 movies of all-time

Alice in Wonderland/Alice Through the Looking Glass

I grew up watching Tim Burton movies and seeing different renditions of Alice in Wonderland, so when I first saw these I knew I'd watch them more than once. I also went to see the second one in the cinema which was a great experience.

Howl's Moving Castle

Any movie by Studio Ghibli really, but this was the first one I watched and the atmosphere, plot and characters make it my favourite amongst them.

The Transformers Movies

Seeing them transform with more detail than the animated series made them memorable. The cgi was well done and the battles were good throughout all of them.
